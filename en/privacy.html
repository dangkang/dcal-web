Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-11

Summary

dCal does not transmit any data from your device.

1. Information We Collect

This app does not collect any personal information, usage information, or schedule content from users.

  • No user registration required
  • No account credentials requested
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising SDKs embedded
  • No device identifiers or location data accessed

2. Data Storage

All schedule data (.cal files) is stored only in locations chosen by you — your local disk, iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive, etc. Neither the app developer nor any third party has access unless you place the files on a service that does.

Settings (appearance mode, last opened file reference, etc.) are stored in the standard UserDefaults and security-scoped bookmarks within the app's sandbox, and are not transmitted externally.

3. Network Communication

The app does not include any network communication capability. macOS App Sandbox does not grant the app any network entitlement, so connecting to external servers is technically blocked at the OS level — this is not a policy promise, it's an OS-enforced constraint.

If you choose to keep your .cal file inside an iCloud Drive or Dropbox folder, the file will be synced through that service in the normal way. That data handling falls under the corresponding service's own privacy policy, not dCal's.

4. Sharing With Third Parties

Since nothing is collected, nothing is shared.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not target any specific age group, nor does it intentionally collect personal information about children (or anyone).

6. Policy Changes

If this policy is revised, changes will be announced via app updates. Please check the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please email info@dangkang.com.
